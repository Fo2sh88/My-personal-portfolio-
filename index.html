About Me

Hello! I'm Mohamed, a Python AI Developer with a unique blend of technical expertise and public sector experience. I enjoy creating things that live on the internet, whether that be websites, applications, or anything in between.

My journey into tech wasn't traditional. For over a decade, I honed my skills in analytical problem-solving, stakeholder management, and data-driven decision-making while serving with Swedish government agencies. I learned to dissect complex situations, communicate intricate concepts clearly, and deliver results under regulatory frameworks.

Fast-forward to today, I'm completing my Python AI Developer education at Teknikhögskolan in Gothenburg, with hands-on experience in machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision. My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences at Linköping University for a variety of clients.
